Music Group Class

Musikgarten ® (Early Childhood Programme)
  • Musikgarten’s core principle – all children are musical
  • Music and movement are a natural, joyous part of childhood
  • Language development, self expression, memory skills, concentration, social interaction, fine motor skills, listening, problem solving, team goal setting and coordination are all impacted by early music and movement education
  • Deepen a child’s appreciation for music and the natural world around him/her, building a foundation for life-long music making
  • Develop musical awareness before playing any instruments 6. Must be introduced early and must involve family
  • Parents’ bonding time with their child
  • Family Music for Babies (Newborn to 18 months)
  • Family Music for Toddlers (18 months to 3 1⁄2 years)
  • Cycle of Seasons (ages 3 to 5)
  • Music Makers: At The Keyboard (from age 6)

Piano

The most ideal instrument to start your musical journey
  • Play (almost) everything regardless of genre or style
  • Enable players to acquire a good general music understanding
  • Trains players to play both hands independently, which can be an advantage when learning a new instrument
  • Choose between classical and pop piano

Guitar

The best instrument due to versatility, cost and portability
  • Lessons focus on reading music notation, finger placement, strumming, sight reading, chord progression and understanding groove
  • Play acoustic or electric guitar covering songs from classic rock to modern pop songs
  • Be a fingerstyle guitarist by learning classical guitar

Vocal

(0-10 years old)
  • Human voice is the ultimate melodic instrument, capable of instant expression with no instrument required to translate thoughts and feelings into sound
  • Find out what your true vocal and singing capacity really is, unlock your untapped vocal potential
  • Learn proper vocal techniques, breath control, and vocal projection, tone shaping
  • Learn how to feel, find and sing in your full vocal range with confidence
