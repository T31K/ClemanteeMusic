Musikgarten

Musikgarten is the quintessential way to develop your child's language skills, self expression, memory skills & concentration. Through team activities, children can also develop their teamwork, coordination and ability to mingle with others.
  • Musikgarten’s core principle -all children are musical
  • Deepen a child’s appreciation for music and the natural world around him/her, building a foundation for life-long music making
  • Develop musical awareness before playing any instruments
  • Great way for parents to bond with their child
